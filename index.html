Who We Are

Experienced researchers,
dedicated to your success

TRes Consulting is a specialist research support service led by a team with extensive academic and clinical research experience. With backgrounds spanning systematic reviews, observational research design, biostatistics, and scientific writing, our team has worked alongside graduate students and early-career researchers across medicine, public health, and life sciences.

Our lead consultant holds advanced postgraduate qualifications in clinical research and has contributed to publications across a range of peer-reviewed international journals. The team brings real-world experience in navigating the full research cycle — not just the methodology, but the practical realities of tight deadlines, supervisor expectations, and journal requirements.

We understand what graduate students face. Our role is not to do your research for you — it is to guide, teach, and support you at every step so that you come out the other side a more capable, more confident researcher.

What We Do

Full-cycle research support

Whether you need help at a single stage or end-to-end guidance through your entire thesis or dissertation, we offer targeted expertise across all phases of the research process.

🎓

ACRP Exam Training & Consulting

Targeted preparation for ACRP certification exams (CCRC, CCRA, CPI). Personalised study plans, domain-by-domain review, practice questions, and one-on-one consulting sessions to accelerate your clinical research career.

🔍

Literature Search & Review

Comprehensive database searches across PubMed, Embase, Scopus, Web of Science, and more. Boolean strategy design, deduplication, and PRISMA-compliant documentation so your search is replicable and defensible.

📋

Screening & Study Selection

Title/abstract and full-text screening with clearly defined inclusion and exclusion criteria. PRISMA flow diagrams and inter-rater reliability checks where required.

🗂️

Data Extraction

Structured extraction of quantitative and qualitative data from primary studies. Custom extraction forms, quality assessment tools (Cochrane RoB, Newcastle-Ottawa), and evidence tables.

📊

Meta-Analysis

Effect size pooling, heterogeneity analysis (I², τ²), subgroup and sensitivity analyses, and publication bias assessment (funnel plots, Egger's test). Produced using RevMan, R, or STATA.

🔬

Primary Data Collection

Survey design, questionnaire development and validation, case report form (CRF) creation, and advice on sampling strategies, ethical approvals, and data management plans.

📈

Statistical Analysis & Interpretation

Descriptive statistics, regression modelling, survival analysis, and diagnostic test accuracy. We help you choose the right test, run the analysis, and — crucially — interpret and present your results with confidence.

✍️

Manuscript Writing & Editing

Drafting and editing of manuscripts from Introduction to Discussion. Scientific clarity, logical structure, and argument coherence — written to the conventions of your field.

📰

Journal Selection & Formatting

Matching your work to the most appropriate journal, formatting manuscripts to exact author guidelines, preparing cover letters, and guiding you through the submission process.

🔄

Revision & Peer Review Response

Structured, point-by-point responses to reviewer comments. Revision of the manuscript and rebuttal letters that are respectful, thorough, and strategically effective.
